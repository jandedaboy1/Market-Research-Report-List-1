<p><p>The electric pool heaters market consists of various types, primarily focusing on constant temperature furnaces and heat pumps. Constant temperature furnaces rapidly heat water to a desired temperature, ideal for quick usage or smaller pools. In contrast, heat pumps extract heat from the air, providing energy-efficient heating, especially in moderate climates. While heat pumps are generally slower to heat, they maintain consistent temperatures over time, making them cost-effective for larger pools and extended use.</p></p>
