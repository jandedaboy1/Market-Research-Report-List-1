<p><p>The spray foam sealant market comprises two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component spray foam is pre-packaged and easy to use, relying on moisture in the air to cure, making it suitable for small projects and DIY applications. In contrast, two-component spray foam requires mixing two components before application, leading to a more durable and higher-performance solution ideal for larger projects and professional use. Both types offer excellent insulation and air sealing properties, enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
