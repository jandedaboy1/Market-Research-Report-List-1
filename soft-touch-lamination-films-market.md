<p><strong>The Soft Touch Lamination Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Side Lamination Film</li><li>Double-Side Lamination Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soft Touch Lamination Films market comprises two primary types: One-Side Lamination Film and Double-Side Lamination Film. One-Side Lamination Film is applied to only one face of a substrate, enhancing its tactile feel and providing a matte finish, making it ideal for products like book covers and packaging. In contrast, Double-Side Lamination Film covers both surfaces, offering durability and an appealing aesthetic, suitable for high-end prints and promotional materials where both sides need protection and a luxurious finish.</p></p>
