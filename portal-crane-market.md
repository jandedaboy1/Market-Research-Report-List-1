<p><p>The portal crane market includes various types designed for specific lifting needs, prominently featuring gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes consist of a complete framework supported by legs, allowing for robust outdoor operation and handling heavy loads, ideal for shipping yards and construction sites. In contrast, half-gantry cranes have one leg fixed and the other supported by an overhead structure, providing versatility in tighter spaces while maintaining significant lifting capacity, commonly used in warehouses and manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
