<p><ul><li>Discrete Controller</li><li>System on Chip (SoC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-paper Display (EPD) control chip market is primarily segmented into two types: Discrete Controllers and System on Chip (SoC) solutions. Discrete controllers consist of separate components designed to manage EPD operations, allowing for flexibility in design but often requiring additional integration efforts. In contrast, SoC solutions integrate the control circuitry within a single chip, optimizing space and power efficiency, which simplifies implementation for manufacturers while providing enhanced performance and reducing overall system complexity in EPD applications.</p></p>
