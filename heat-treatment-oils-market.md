<p><ul><li>Cold Oils</li><li>Semi-hot Oils</li><li>Hot Oils</li><li>Bright Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat treatment oils market is categorized into four main types based on their operating temperatures and applications. Cold oils are typically used for low-temperature quenching processes, providing minimal thermal shock. Semi-hot oils operate at moderate temperatures, enhancing mechanical properties without excessive distortion. Hot oils facilitate high-temperature quenching and tempering, ensuring thorough hardening and surface finish. Bright oils, known for their low volatility and excellent thermal stability, are used for high-quality surface treatments, ensuring a polished final product.</p></p>
