<p><strong>The Fire-Proof Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type Sealants</li><li>Intumescent Type Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-proof sealant market comprises two primary types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and rubber-like, providing excellent adhesion and movement accommodation, making them suitable for dynamic environments. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char that insulates and protects underlying materials from fire. Both types are essential in maintaining structural integrity and ensuring fire safety in various construction applications, each serving specific protective functions against fire hazards.</p></p>
