<p><p>The electrode holder market is categorized into insulated and non-insulated types. Insulated electrode holders are designed with materials that prevent electrical conduction, enhancing safety and user protection during welding operations. They are ideal for environments where handling is frequent and safety is paramount. Non-insulated holders, on the other hand, provide direct electrical contact with the electrode, making them suitable for specific applications where insulation is not a concern. The choice between the two depends on operational requirements and safety considerations.</p></p>
