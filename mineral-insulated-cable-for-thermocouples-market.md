Simplex cables consist of a single thermocouple wire pair, ideal for standard temperature measurements. Duplex cables feature two pairs, allowing for more complex applications such as redundancy or simultaneous readings. Other types may include specialized configurations tailored for unique environments or specific industrial requirements, enhancing measurement accuracy and reliability in demanding conditions.</p></p>
