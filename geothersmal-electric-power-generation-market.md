<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The GeOthersmal Electric Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Steam Stations</li><li>Flash Steam Power Stations</li><li>Binary Cycle Stations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geothermal electric power generation encompasses three main market types: </p><p>1. **Dry Steam Stations** extract steam directly from geothermal reservoirs to drive turbines, offering efficient energy conversion. </p><p>2. **Flash Steam Power Stations** use high-pressure hot water, which is allowed to "flash" into steam when pressure drops, generating electricity. </p><p>3. **Binary Cycle Stations** transfer heat from geothermal water to a secondary fluid with a lower boiling point, enabling electricity production at lower temperatures, while conserving geothermal resources. Each type contributes uniquely to sustainable energy production.</p></p>
