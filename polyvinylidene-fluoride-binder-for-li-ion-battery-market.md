<p><p>Polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) binders play a crucial role in lithium-ion battery production, enhancing electrode performance and stability. The market for PVDF binders is divided into two main types based on their manufacturing processes: suspension polymerization and emulsion polymerization. Suspension polymerization yields larger PVDF particles, which improve viscosity and processing properties, while emulsion polymerization produces smaller particles that enhance dispersion and adhesion in battery electrodes. Both methods contribute uniquely to the efficiency and performance of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
