<p><p>Electrical safety mats are categorized based on voltage levels: low, medium, and high voltage. Low voltage mats are designed for environments with a maximum of 1000 volts, providing basic protection for personnel working near electrical equipment. Medium voltage mats, operating between 1kV to 35kV, offer enhanced protection for more hazardous environments, typically seen in industrial settings. High voltage mats are utilized for systems exceeding 35kV, ensuring maximum insulation and protection in high-risk electrical areas, necessary for safeguarding both workers and equipment.</p></p>
