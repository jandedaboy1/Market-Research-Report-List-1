<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber-Based DSS</li><li>Multimode Fiber-Based DSS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Strain Sensing (DSS) systems utilize optical fibers to measure strain and deformation along their length. The market is categorized into single-mode fiber-based DSS and multimode fiber-based DSS. Single-mode fiber-based DSS is favored for long-distance applications due to its superior sensitivity and lower signal loss, making it ideal for infrastructure monitoring. In contrast, multimode fiber-based DSS offers cost-effectiveness and is suitable for shorter distances, often used in industrial applications where extensive spatial resolution is required.</p></p>
