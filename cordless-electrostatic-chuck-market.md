<p><p>The cordless electrostatic chuck market comprises two main types: polyimide electrostatic chucks and ceramic electrostatic chucks. Polyimide electrostatic chucks are lightweight and flexible, offering excellent thermal stability and adherence to various substrates. They are commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing processes. In contrast, ceramic electrostatic chucks provide superior rigidity and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. Both types serve to securely hold wafers in place during processing, enhancing efficiency and precision in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
