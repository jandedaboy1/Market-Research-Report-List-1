<p><p>The automobile anti-fog coating market comprises two primary types: UV curing and thermal curing. UV curing involves the application of a coating that is hardened using ultraviolet light, providing a quick drying time and enhanced durability. This method is often preferred for its efficiency in mass production. In contrast, thermal curing utilizes heat to catalyze the curing process, resulting in a robust finish suitable for diverse climatic conditions. Both methods aim to improve visibility and safety by preventing fogging on vehicle surfaces.</p></p>
