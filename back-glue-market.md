<p><strong>The Back Glue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1 Component Paste BG</li><li>1 Component Liquid BG</li><li>2 Component BG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Back Glue (BG) market includes three main types: 1-Component Paste BG, 1-Component Liquid BG, and 2-Component BG. 1-Component Paste BG is a pre-mixed adhesive in paste form, easy to apply and ideal for bonding rigid materials. 1-Component Liquid BG is a ready-to-use liquid adhesive, suitable for flexible substrates and quick-drying applications. 2-Component BG consists of a resin and hardener mixed before use, offering superior strength and durability for demanding industrial applications. Each type caters to specific bonding needs across industries.</p></p>
