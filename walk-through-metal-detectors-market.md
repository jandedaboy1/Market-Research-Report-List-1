<p><p>Walk-through metal detectors are crucial for security applications and are categorized into two main types: single zone and multi-zone. </p><p>Single zone detectors feature a uniform scanning area, offering a basic level of detection for metal objects. In contrast, multi-zone detectors utilize multiple detection zones that allow for more precise localization of metallic items, enhancing accuracy and reducing false alarms. This makes them particularly effective in high-security environments, where detailed monitoring and rapid identification of threats are essential.</p></p>
