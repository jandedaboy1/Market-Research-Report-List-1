<p><p>DC Voltage Monitoring Relays are essential for ensuring the stability and safety of electrical systems by continuously monitoring voltage levels. The market comprises two primary types: Single-Phase and Three-Phase relays. Single-Phase relays are designed for applications with a single voltage source, commonly used in residential and small commercial setups. In contrast, Three-Phase relays monitor three interconnected voltage sources, ideal for industrial systems where balanced load distribution is crucial. Both types enhance operational reliability and prevent equipment damage due to voltage fluctuations.</p></p>
