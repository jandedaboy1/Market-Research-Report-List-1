<p><p>The PTZ IP cameras market is categorized into centralized and decentralized types. Centralized IP cameras operate through a central server where all video feeds are processed and stored, offering robust management and control. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras distribute processing tasks across individual cameras, enabling them to operate independently and reduce reliance on centralized systems. This differentiation impacts scalability, network architecture, and storage solutions, making each type suitable for specific security needs and infrastructure setups.</p></p>
